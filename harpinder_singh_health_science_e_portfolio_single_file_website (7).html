About Me

Hi, my name is Harpinder Singh, and I am currently pursuing a degree in Health Science at Indiana University Indianapolis. I am someone who values helping others, staying focused, and growing through real experience. Throughout high school and my clinical rotations, I learned how meaningful patient care can be—not just physically, but emotionally too. My long-term goal is to continue building clinical experience, expand my knowledge of patient care, and become a healthcare professional who makes a positive difference in people’s lives.
